Meet the Heads behind Headzup

The two unstoppable forces

The Creative Dynamo

Akhil , our vibrant co-founder, is a marketing maverick with a heart full of ideas and a knack for turning dreams into reality.

Fuelled by boundless creativity and a passion for innovation , our young champ is determined to make Headzup the go-to brand for adventurous kids everywhere. His youthful energy and keen marketing instincts keep the brand fresh, exciting, and always ahead of the curve.

The Perpetual Pioneer

Naren , our seasoned co-founder, hails from the prestigious IIT-Delhi. Never settling, always striving, Naren is the driving force behind our relentless pursuit of excellence .

He’s still hungry for innovation, constantly pushing the envelope to create something that marries safety with style in ways you’ve never seen before. His experience and vision ensure that every Headzup helmet is a perfect blend of cutting-edge technology and fashionable design.

Together, They’re Headzup

United by a shared passion for excellence and a commitment to never stop learning, Akhil and Naren have embarked on a mission to redefine what it means to be a fun brand for kids.

Each Headzup helmet is crafted with meticulous care , from eco-friendly materials to cutting-edge design, ensuring every adventure is both safe and sensational.
